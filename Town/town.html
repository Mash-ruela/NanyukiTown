A Town Beneath the Peak

Nanyuki sits at the foot of Mount Kenya, Africa's second-highest peak. Founded in 1907, it is a hub for tourism, agriculture and trade in Laikipia County. The town straddles the Equator and enjoys a cool highland climate of 17Â°C year-round.

Surrounded by world-class conservancies, strawberry farms and icy trout streams, Nanyuki is one of Kenya's most beloved highland towns.
